Our Story

Beauty as a
Discipline

Maison Vernis was born from the belief that nail care is not maintenance — it is artistry. Founded in the heart of New York by master nail technician Élise Fontaine, our atelier treats every appointment as a private performance, where craft and quiet luxury converge.

We source only toxin-free, cruelty-free lacquers from Europe's finest ateliers. Our technicians train for 400 hours before touching a single client. The result? Nails that look like couture and last like architecture.
